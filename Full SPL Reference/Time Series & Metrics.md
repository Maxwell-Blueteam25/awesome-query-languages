### **V. Time Series & Metrics**

_Handling data over time._

|**Command**|**Description**|**Example**|
|---|---|---|
|**`timechart`**|Creates a time-series table. Limit: 1 dimension in `by` clause.|`|
|**`bin`**|Buckets continuous data (time or numbers) into discrete groups.|`|
|**`mstats`**|**Metrics Only.** Similar to stats but for metric indexes.|`|
|**`mcatalog`**|**Metrics Only.** Lists metric names.|`|
