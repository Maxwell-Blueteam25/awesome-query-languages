### **VI. Formatting & Visualization**

The "Presentation" phase: `render` operator.

|**Command**|**Description**|**Example**|
|---|---|---|
|**`render barchart`**|Renders a bar chart.|`|
|**`render timechart`**|Renders a line chart over time.|`|
|**`render piechart`**|Renders a pie chart.|`|
|**`render scatterchart`**|Renders points (often for maps).|`render scatterchart with (kind=map)`.|
|**`render anomalychart`**|Visualizes detected anomalies.|`render anomalychart with (anomalycolumns=anomalies)`.|
